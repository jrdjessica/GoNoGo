Welcome to GoNoGo!

Have you ever felt obligated to attend an event, all while wondering if everyone else will show up?
Have you ever said yes to an event you didn't want to attend? You found GoNoGo!

Once you create an event, you can invite your friends to a decision-making process free from social pressure. When invited, you have the choice to attend or skip out anonymously.

Here's the kicker: if the majority decides to go, the event is a go! But if the majority declines, we'll automatically cancel the event.
Join us today and take the hassle out of event planning!
